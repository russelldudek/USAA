## Typography decision
The 2025 Annual Report uses a compact, modern humanist/grotesk sans system with condensed, high-impact headings and dense but readable body copy. No authoritative public typeface specification or reusable licensed USAA font files were located. The implementation uses locally available Noto Sans/Liberation Sans with a Liberation Sans Narrow/Arial Narrow heading stack as licensed structural substitutes. No proprietary font binaries are copied or committed.

## Visual grammar
The employer-recognition layer uses USAA navy, white space, strong condensed headlines, crisp red rules, selective gold emphasis, editorial asymmetry, and restrained geometry. The candidate-original layer is the synchronized two-plane Automation Assurance Twin, not a recreation of a USAA product interface.

## Photography and imagery
The campaign does not reuse member, employee, facility, or military photography. Recognition comes from the official identity, source-sampled palette, annual-report typography behavior, and original role-derived diagrams. This avoids implying Russell's involvement in pictured USAA experiences or outcomes.

## Use boundary
This is an independent candidate work product. It is not an official USAA publication and does not imply affiliation, endorsement, authorship, or access to nonpublic information. Source-control and publication infrastructure are intentionally excluded from recruiter-facing surfaces.
