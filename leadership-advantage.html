Leadership Advantage Brief

The selection thesis

The platform can be learned. The operating system has to be led. USAA gains the most from a director who can connect process engineering, technology, risk, economics, people, and member outcomes - then make that system repeatable.

Enterprise operating range

  • Process and controls: quality systems, root cause, standard work, measures, decision rights, and high-reliability execution.
  • Scale and cadence: 24/7 leadership routines, throughput visibility, escalation, Lean/Gemba practice, and frontline coaching.
  • Automation and AI: opportunity framing, workflow design, human authority, evaluation, telemetry, and governance rails.
  • Transformation leadership: cross-functional integration across operations, engineering, quality, ERP, CRM, support, and customer workflows.

Mandate fit

  • Align BPM leaders, Process Engineers, process owners, risk, technology, and finance around the same portfolio decisions.
  • Select tools from process truth, business need, control requirements, and operating economics.
  • Build business cases tied to member value, employee flow, quality, risk, adoption, and maintenance burden.
  • Turn successful automations into standards, coaching capability, reusable patterns, and measurable realized value.

What USAA gains

Better selection

Weak candidates are redesigned early; strong candidates receive clear ownership and evidence.

Stronger adoption

Automation is built into real work, leader cadence, training, and frontline accountability.

Visible assurance

Human authority, controls, monitoring, fallback, and value ownership are legible before scale.

Fast-start model

  1. Learn the current portfolio, platforms, risk obligations, process-intelligence capability, and decision rights.
  2. Apply the Automation Assurance Twin to a bounded mix of promising, stalled, and underperforming cases.
  3. Coach Process Engineers and partners through evidence-backed decisions using real work.
  4. Convert proven patterns into standards, platform guidance, and a quarterly value-and-control review.

Why this background is differentiated

Many candidates will know an automation tool. Fewer have led the full operating system around the tool: the process, controls, people, cadence, economics, escalation, and adoption required to make automation sustainable across an enterprise. That is the advantage Russell brings.
